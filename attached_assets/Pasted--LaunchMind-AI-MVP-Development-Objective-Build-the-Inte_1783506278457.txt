# Intelligence Modules

Create the following independent modules.

1. Launch Readiness Engine

Input:

LaunchContext

Output:

- launchReadinessScore
- readinessLevel
- decision

- GO
- GO WITH RISKS
- NO-GO

Reuse existing analysis information.

Do not call Gemini.

---

2. Timeline Intelligence

Compute:

- analysisAge
- contextFreshness
- staleDataWarning

Return:

timelineHealth

timelineMessage

3. Risk Intelligence

Analyze existing risk information.

Return:

- overallRisk
- criticalRisks
- mediumRisks
- lowRisks
- riskSummary

Use existing Risk Assessment only.

Do not generate new risks.

---

4. Recommendation Engine

Aggregate recommendations already present.

Return:

- executiveRecommendation
- priorityActions
- blockers
- nextSteps

Reuse existing AI Recommendations.

Do not generate new recommendations.

---

5. Confidence Engine

Compute:

overallConfidence

ConfidenceLevel

- HIGH
- MEDIUM
- LOW

- analysis completeness
- history availability
- integration availability
